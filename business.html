EARTH HOUR FOR BUSINESS

Find out how your business or organisation can get involved this Earth Hour.

Since 2007, Earth Hour has been embraced by communities, large and small. Are you representing a community, organisation, landmark or council? This year, we invite you to take #TimeOutForNature and switch off at 8.30pm on Saturday 25 March 2023.

Register your participation in Earth Hour, and then join hundreds of communities, organisations, landmarks and councils that want to see stronger action on climate change and nature loss by sending us your logo to appear on the pledge wall below.

There are many ways your community can get involved in Earth Hour:
  • Sign up to switch off and take #TimeOutForNature this Earth Hour.
  • Take part in the movement online. Click here for social media resources and more to show the world you're switching off this Earth Hour.
  • Sign up and host a fundraiser this Earth Hour.

Promise for the planet

We are joining hundreds of businesses across Australia to switch off for Earth Hour 2023 for the future of our planet. We proudly stand as part of a community that wants to take #TimeOutForNature and join the global movement calling for stronger action on climate change and nature loss.

Business as usual isn’t enough. We need to accelerate our efforts to limit the dangerous impact of climate change and transition to zero carbon emissions. We intend to be part of the solution to ensure that the natural resources and ecosystems that underpin our organisation are used sustainably.

We pledge our support for a renewables future so that Australia can become a renewable energy superpower. Together we will help protect the planet for future generations.
